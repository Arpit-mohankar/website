About Us

We aim that no student should be unknown about the opportunities going on let it be open source events,hackathons,internships etc.We also aim that, in this pandemic every student must learn some skills and showcase it into their resume.Through this community we want to reach each and every student.

But Why Us?

  • You will have access to tons of free resources.
  • Updates about events,hackathons,internships etc.
  • We will be planning some webinars from the experts so that you can get knowledge about technologies etc.
  • We will be keeping some community meetings where we can address our queries.
  • You guys can add new skills to your resume.
  • You will get some knowledge about how to build a strong resume,LinkedIn and github(it's the best thing).
Do we have to invest money?
No!!!! Just invest your time that would be enough. Remember it's the community by the students,for the students and of the students.
